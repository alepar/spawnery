## Terminal Color Schemes

Color schemes were sourced from the **iTerm2-Color-Schemes** collection by Mark Badolato
(<https://github.com/mbadolato/iTerm2-Color-Schemes>). The Windows Terminal JSON variants were
used as the basis for generating xterm-compatible palettes (see `scripts/schemes/`). Individual
schemes retain their original authors. Hex color palettes are reproduced as data; scheme names
are preserved as-is.
